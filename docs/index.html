Fashion Intelligence is a daily report on the global apparel, footwear, accessories, luxury, and streetwear markets, with a focus on the United States and the style hubs of New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago. Each edition tracks five things: the current season's trends (colors, silhouettes, fabrics, patterns), next-season predictions, the most-hyped releases and emerging designers, consumer-spending behavior, and streetwear. Every claim is sourced and linked; rumors are labeled; market figures are directional. Updated each day.
